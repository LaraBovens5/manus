Specializations

  • Physiotherapy

    Physiotherapy focuses on addressing acute and chronic movement problems of the musculoskeletal system.

    Learn more

  • Manual Therapy

    Manual therapy, a specialization within physiotherapy which involves a thorough analysis and specific treatment of the muscle and joint system including mobilizations and manipulations.

    Learn more

  • Orthopedic and Sports Rehabilitation

    After surgery or a sports injury, an active approach is crucial. Movement promotes the recovery process and is central to our treatment. We complement this active approach with manual techniques to achieve the best possible outcome.

    Learn more

  • Headache and temporomandibular disorder

    Common headaches and temporomandibular disorders can significantly impact your quality of life. Our manual therapist can help alleviate these problems.

    Learn more

  • Dizziness & BPPV

    Are you experiencing dizziness with specific movements or when turning your head? Do you have balance problems after a neck injuy? Our specific tests determine whether you suffer from BPPV (Benign Paroxysmal Positional Vertigo) or cervicogenic dizziness, and we offer targeted treatments for these issues.

    Learn more

  • Hand Rehabilitation

    Hand rehabilitation is crucial after hand injuries, surgery, or other conditions. This specialization aims to restore hand and wrist function, mobility, strength, and endurance.

    Learn more
