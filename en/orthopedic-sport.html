Orthopedic and Sports Rehabilitation

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Whether you've recently undergone surgery or experienced a period of immobilization, it's important to regain mobility, muscle strength, and functionality as quickly as possible.

A personalised exercise program, tailor-made for each patient forms the basis of the recovery process. This can be supplemented with manual techniques for the most effective results.

What can you contact Manus for?

  • Prostheses (e.g., knee, shoulder, thumb, hip...)
  • Arthroscopies (e.g., meniscus, shoulder...)
  • Tendon surgeries (e.g., Rotator cuff, Achilles tendon...)
  • Spinal surgeries (stenosis, disc hernia...)
  • Fractures
  • ACL surgery (anterior cruciate ligament surgery)

Sports Rehabilitation

Manus physiotherapy clinic offers an active approach for the treatment and prevention of sports injuries. We create customized rehabilitation plans by combining sport-specific exercises with manual treatment techniques, adapted to the individual athlete and their sport.

Our guidance includes a return to sports and competition, with a special focus on preventing recurring injuries. Prevention is a central focus in our practice. Therefore, we have the right equipment to work on conditioning, balance, flexibility, and strength improvement.

What can you contact Manus for?

  • Lower limb (e.g., anterior cruciate ligament injury, ankle sprains, knee injuries, Achilles tendon tendinopathy...)
  • Upper limb (Rotator cuff tendinopathy, tennis elbow...)
  • General conditioning
  • Sport-specific training
  • Fractures
