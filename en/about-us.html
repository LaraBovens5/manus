About us

Welcome to Manus Physiotherapy Practice: where your well-being takes center stage

Are you in search of professional care and guidance for your body and health? Then you've come to the right place at Manus. Following my studies in physiotherapy at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel and specialization as a manual therapist, I proudly established my own physiotherapy practice, Manus, in 2023.

Where mind meets body

Manus places a strong emphasis on sports and embracing a healthy lifestyle. As a physiotherapist, I firmly believe that a healthy body and a healthy mind go hand in hand. We not only address issues but also actively seek their causes and promote recovery, always in collaboration with you as the patient.

Working together towards a healthier lifestyle

Much like I value my sportive background, I am a team player in my professional practice. Collaboration is at the core of my work as a physiotherapist. I place importance on a healthy lifestyle and find satisfaction in activities such as crossfit and cooking. This is the vision I aim to share with my patients.

Tailored comprehensive care

Manus Physiotherapy Practice ensures a personalized approach. We combine joint and muscle treatments with customized exercise therapy to offer a comprehensive package entirely tailored to the patient. Our goal: to guide you on your journey toward better health and quality of life.
