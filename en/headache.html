Headache and temporomandibular disorder

Headache

Lower back pain and headaches are common conditions, but headaches are often underdiagnosed. Whether it's migraine, tension headaches, or cervicogenic headaches, our manual therapist offers tailored treatments.

Manual techniques reduce muscle tension, supported by exercise therapy for muscle strengthening and posture correction. This combined approach promotes effective recovery, with the primary goal of resuming an active social life including relaxation.

For which headaches can you contact Manus?

  • Tension headache
  • Migraine
  • Cervicogenic headache

Temporomandibular disorder

Temporomandibular disorders (TMJ), problems with the jaw joint and surrounding muscles, have substantial consequences for daily life. From speaking to chewing, these issues range from a clicking sensation when opening the mouth to pain, headaches, and neck pain.

Despite the complexity of the joint, we can quickly provide relief through manual techniques, complemented by exercise therapy to ensure a long-term effect.
