Praktical information

What to Bring to Your First Appointment:

  • Your electronic identity card
  • Your health insurance sticker
  • A medical prescription from your doctor
  • Optionally, the details of your insurance provider
  • Medical imaging, such as X-rays, ultrasounds, MRIs, and their corresponding reports

Payment for each consultation is made in cash. Electronic payments are only possible through the Bancontact app.

Please note that appointments not canceled in a timely manner may incur charges.

Rates and Reimbursement

If you have a prescription from your general practitioner or specialist, you may be eligible for reimbursement according to the rules of the RIZIV.

It's important to note that the therapists in this practice are not part of the national convention of physiotherapists.

GDPR

If you wish to obtain more information about the privacy statement regarding the protection of personal data and health information, you can request it at any time.
